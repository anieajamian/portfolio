Web Design

SeedInvest

A redesign concept for SeedInvest, an NYC equity-crowdfunding startup that curates which companies appear on its platform. The visual concept centered on the paper airplane: great ideas start on the back of a napkin, and a paper airplane evokes childhood, early ideas, simplicity, and experimentation — there's no single right way to fold one, just like there's no single right way to build a startup.
